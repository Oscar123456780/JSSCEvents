About this event

This event will be hosted on the Holden Street Courts on certain Lunches in Term 3 and Term 4. It is organised by the JS House Captains. It will be following the official NBA basketball rulebook.

Important rules:

  • Held Ball: In the case of a 'Held Ball,' the offensive team (team without possession) will gain possession.
  • Scoring: 1 point for penalty shot, two points for regular shot and three points when a shot is made with both of the player's feet behind the three point line.
  • Injuries and Fighting: Injuries and fights will be resolved by the referee.
  • Referee rights: Anyone who argues or disobeys the referee will be prosecuted accordingly. The referee makes the final call to any issues. There will be 2-3 referees in a game; 1 main referee and 1-2 assistants.
