About this event

This event will be hosted on the Holden Street Courts on certain Lunches in Term 3 and Term 4. It is organised by the JS House Captains. It will be following the official NBA basketball rulebook.

Important rules:

  • Held Ball: In the case of a 'Held Ball,' the initial attacking team will continue possession, unless they 'travel,' or a 'foul' occurs. Judges and assistants will ensure that this is a fair event.
  • Scoring: 1 point for penalty shot, two points for regular shot and three points when a shot is made with both of the player's feet behind the three point line.
  • Injuries and Fighting: Injuries and fights will be resolved by the 'Main Referee.'
  • According to NBA rules, the attacking team has 24 seconds to attempt a shot, or a whistle will be called and the defensive team will gain possession.
  • According to NBA rules, A player shall not remain in the opponent’s restricted area (key) for more than 3 consecutive seconds while his/her team is in control of a live ball in the frontcourt and the game clock is running.

  • The 'Main Referee' is capable of giving warnings to players who show unacceptable behaviour.
  • The 'Game' starts as soon as the timekeeper signals that the game is on.
  • 'Team Supervisors' are responsible for sorting out all subsitutions and timeouts.
  • Referee rights: Anyone who argues or disobeys the referee will be prosecuted accordingly. The referee makes the final call to any issues. There will be 2-3 referees in a game; 1 main referee and 1-2 assistants.
